While we endevour to meet all dietary needs, we would like to be open with what we're offering to ensure you can make your best decisions regarding any diet, cultural or personal needs.



Morning and Afternoon tea



We will provide a selection of light morning and afternoon refreshments, including fruit, muesli bars, and assorted snacks. For those with larger appetites, we recommend bringing additional food.
Tea and coffee will be available, along with numerous water refill stations. We encourage you to bring a reusable water bottle or keep cup to support our sustainability initiatives.



Lunch

For lunches, we will have available Dominos Pizza including gluten free, vegan and vegetarian pizza from Domino's (details on ingredients here and their vegan 'impossible' pizzas here)

For those where this may not fit your dietary needs, we suggest bringing in your lunch. Food purchasing options are also available as below.



Food Purchasing options

A cafe is located at building 205 on the campus map.



Prayer space

A Multifaith room is available on campus, located at 208 G on the campus map.
