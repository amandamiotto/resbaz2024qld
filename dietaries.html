Morning and Afternoon teas



We will provide a selection of light morning and afternoon refreshments, including fruit, muesli bars, and assorted snacks. For those with larger appetites, we recommend bringing additional food.
Tea and coffee will be available, along with numerous water refill stations. We encourage you to bring a reusable water bottle or keep cup to support our sustainability initiatives.

Lunches

For lunches, we will have available sandwiches, including vegetarian sandwiches, Dominos pizza including gluten free, vegan and vegetarian pizza (details on ingredients here) and red rooster subs (who have information about their halal certified chicken and practices here).
