How to get to Resbaz?


Resbaz will be at ACU Nudgee campus, in Brisbane(ACU Maps Link).





Getting to campus


ACU Banyo is accessible by public transport and cycle routes. Free parking is also available on campus.


Full details can be found on the ACU Website here.

Public transport

For the convenience of students and staff, ACU runs two free bus services to local public transport hubs.

The ACU service runs Monday to Friday between Toombul Shopping Centre, Northgate train stations and Chermside Shopping Centre precinct. The departure point from ACU is BCC Bus stop 34, near the corner of Approach Rd. and Earnshaw Rd.

The bus routes can vary depending on traffic conditions to ensure adherence, as much as possible, to the timetables. In consideration of safety and scheduling, passengers may only board/disembark at the designated stops.

For the week of Resbaz, the shuttle bus to/from campus start at 7:30 am hourly from Chermside and every 30 min from Toombul/Northgate/ACU loop. Shuttle Bus pickup locations are found here.

Cycling

Brisbane is a cycle-friendly city and our campus is a cycle-friendly place. Look up nearby bicycle paths near campus at the Brisbane City Council Cycling website. When you arrive at campus, you can store your two-wheeled steed at any of the bike racks shown on the campus map and be sure you have your lock/chain too. Racks are located outside of the Library (Bld. 204) adjacent to the covered seating area. Another set is located near P3 (airport side of Bld. 206) . There are showers located in Bld. 208. G (near The Track), Bld. 215 (Pool) and Bld. 216.G adjacent to the bike racks.


Driving and parking

Free parking is available on campus. There is some parking available (P1 & P7) but spaces are limited. Otherwise, there is plenty of parking on ring road parking and at the base of the campus is free.

There are a number of disabled parking spaces on campus for those who hold and display a current government-issued permit.


At campus?

The registration desk can be found in Building 216, Level 1 (ground floor), Room 18 on the campus map.
