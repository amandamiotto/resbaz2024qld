Accessibility bursaries available



The cost of living is making life very tough right now. We don't want anyone to miss out on ResBaz because of financial concerns, so we aim to offer support if you are worried about the cost of attending ResBaz.

If you have concerns about childcare, travel1 or other accessibility expenses, please contact us at resbaz@qcif.edu.au to ask about our Accessibility Bursaries. We're also offering free registration to volunteers, so get in touch resbaz@qcif.edu.au if you're keen to help out.

1. Unfortunately our budget doesn't stretch to travel scholarships, so we can't help with flights or accommodation. We can support extra bus/ferry/train/parking costs and similar that would otherwise put a strain on your budget.
