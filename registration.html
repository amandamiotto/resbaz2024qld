Cost of Living Support



The cost of living is making life very tough right now. We don't want anyone to miss out on ResBaz because of financial concerns, so we aim to offer a free ticket if you are worried about the cost of attending ResBaz.

We're are offering free registration to volunteers and those that need it, so get in touch resbaz@qcif.edu.au if you're keen to help out.
